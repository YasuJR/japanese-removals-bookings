Phase 16 — Unpaid invoice reminder automation

When an invoice is created but unpaid, send SMS reminders after 3, 7, and 14 days from the invoice issue date.

  • Skips Pending, draft invoices, Cancelled, and Paid bookings
  • Stops when payment is received or reminders are cancelled
  • Logs: payment_reminder_1_sent, payment_reminder_2_sent, payment_reminder_3_sent, payment_reminders_cancelled
  • Send payment reminder now and Cancel payment reminders on the booking edit page

Phase 14 — Double booking prevention

Warn when blocking bookings overlap on the same move date. Pending and Cancelled jobs do not block availability.

  • Blocking statuses: Confirmed, On Route, Paid, Completed
  • Time overlap uses start time + duration/finish time
  • Warning banner + override checkbox — never auto-blocks save
  • Pending bookings skip calendar sync until confirmed
  • Logs: double_booking_checked, double_booking_conflict, double_booking_override

Active on save and confirm
